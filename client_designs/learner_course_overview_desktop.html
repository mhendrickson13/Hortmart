About this course

Learn how to design print-ready graphics for T‑shirts: composition, contrast, typography, and practical constraints like fabrics and inks. This course is structured for fast progress with clear milestones.

What you’ll learn
Create strong graphic hierarchy
Make designs readable at small sizes and in real-world print.
Choose colors that print well
Avoid muddy results by planning contrast and inks.
Prepare files for production
Export templates and specs for common print workflows.
